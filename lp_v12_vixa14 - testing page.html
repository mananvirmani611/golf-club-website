The Vixa V12 is designed to launch the ball for you, even on imperfect contact. You get the height and length you need to reach the green in fewer strokes. And you don’t have to spend hours making swing changes to do it.

What makes this possible? It’s the club’s unique, intelligent design…

  • The club face is about an inch shorter than a standard 460cc driver, which promotes a level or slightly descending strike—just like you make with every club except your driver

  • The shorter club face means you tee the ball up lower, which increases your control and consistency.
  • The club’s 14.5 degree loft (4 degrees more than a typical driver) launches the ball up in the air with a naturally high trajectory.
